Our Client

Kaj-Robert and Kurre Räven owns and operates a hair salon on Solsiden, Trondheim. The salon has been passed down for three generations, continuing the Zohan-inspired cutting style. Kaj-Robert aka Kajjan is a good friend of the whole group. Their hair salon is called Cut and Shut and serve men and women of all ages. Kaj-Robert has recently experienced a decrease in customers since last summer. We offered to make him a website to hopefully get his business back on track. The website will be called Cut and Shut.

Purpose and goals

Kaj-Robert needs a website that displays his business in a professional and informal way. He wants information about his salon, along with the different services he provides. He needs his contact information and location easily accessible. The site will hopefully also show inspiration for different hairstyles. Another goal is to make a reservation function that lets customers plan their visits better.

Audience

Our main audience is the locals of Trondheim city. We target people of all ages and aim to have a broad variety of hairstyles available on the website. The audience expects good reservation service along with a professional and easily accessible website. The site should be pleasant to look at and information should be placed so the customer doesn’t have to search for it. They will also want pictures so they easier understand the services provided by the salon.

Content of the site and how it is organized

Navnigation bar

The navbar will contain buttons so the user can quickly navigate between the different pages on the site.

homepage

The homepage will contain a large picture of the salon and some brief information about it. It will act as a “welcome” text to the sites users.

Photogallery

The photo gallery will contain a photo carousel of different cuts the salon has made.

Reservation scheme

This page will contain a form to order a cut.

Footer

The footer will contain links to different social medias.

Functional and Non-Functional Requirements

Functional

  • The website should have a navigation bar to easily get around the different pages.
  • The website should have a responsive design so it works on both pc and mobile phones
  • The website should have the graphical user interface to accept reservations from the user.

Non-functional

  • The website should look appealing to the users
  • The website should download as quickly as possible
  • The code must be written accurately and neatly and lead to the least possible maintenance

Final location

We have not yet decided on where the web page will reside after the project, nor who whould be responsible for maintenance.
