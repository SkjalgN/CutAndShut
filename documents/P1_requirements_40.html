Administrative details

Our Client:

Kaj Robert Børve owns and operates a hair salon on Solsiden, Trondheim. He is the brother of Andreas, one of our group members, and a good friend of the whole group. The hair salon is called Cut and Shut and serve boys and girls of all ages. Kaj-Robert has experienced a decrease in customers since last summer. We offered to make him a website to hopefully get his business back on track. The website will be called “Cut and Shut salon”.

Purpose and goals

Kaj Robert needs a website that displays his business in a professional and informal way. He wants information about his salon, along with the different services he provides. He needs his contact information and location easily accessible. The site will hopefully also show inspiration for different hairstyles where you can filter options like length and color. Another goal is to make a reservation function that lets customers plan their visits better.

Audience

Our main audience is the locals of Trondheim city. We target people of all ages and aim to have a broad variety of hairstyles available on the website. The audience expects good reservation service along with a professional and easily accessible website. The site should be pleasant to look at and information should be placed so the customer doesn’t have to search for it. They will also want pictures so they easier understand the services provided by the salon.

Content of the site and how it is organized

EVERY PAGE

  • “Menu”

    The website will have a side-menu on every page where you can quickly access all the different parts of the website.

  • “Footer”

    The footer will be visible on all pages and contain contact information to the salon.

    • PAGE 1

  • «Header»

    On the front page a full width image of the salon will be displayed with the logo on top.

  • “About”

    Underneath the header there will be an introduction to the site and some information about the salon. It will act as a “welcome” text to the sites users.

  • “Hairstyles”

    This section will contain shortcuts to the inspiration page. It will display the major options like gender and kids/adult with pictures to quickly get to the most useful part of the site.

    • PAGE 2

  • “Header”

    Will contain a header and some information about this page.

  • “Filters”

    This section will give the users the opportunity to filter through different types of hair. Will also contain a search function.

  • “Pictures”

    The rest of the page will be pictures and names of hairstyles which can be filtered by the user to find your preferred hairstyle.

    • PAGE 3

  • “Calendar”

    Will contain a visual calender to see when there are available reservations.

  • “Reservation scheme”

    The user will be able to set a reservation at their preferred time.
